Suppose n companies are coming to your university for placements, with the i-th company having k(i) rounds of interviews where i = {1, 2, 3, ... n}. It is assumed that every round of interview for every company will last for time T. You have S students each applying to multiple companies. Arrange the timings of the rounds of interview so that every candidate can sit for all the rounds of the companies they were selected for.
